Rules & Guidelines
Please read and accept the following rules before using EmojiBot2:
  • No NSFW Content: Do not upload or use NSFW (Not Safe For Work) images as emojis. This includes explicit, pornographic, or inappropriate content.
  • Appropriate Content Only: All emojis must be appropriate for all audiences. Keep content family-friendly.
  • Respect Copyright: Only use images you have permission to use. Do not use copyrighted material without authorization.
  • No Malicious Content: Do not upload images that could harm users' devices or contain malicious code.
  • Follow Community Guidelines: Emojis that violate these rules will be removed, and users may be banned.
